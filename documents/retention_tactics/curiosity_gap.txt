CURIOSITY GAP - Keeping Them Hooked

THE HOOK:
- Start with incomplete information
- Hint at valuable knowledge
- Suggest there's more to learn

CREATING TENSION:
- Build questions without answering
- Use "what if" and "how to"
- Leave gaps in the story

THE PAYOFF:
- Deliver unexpected insights
- Connect dots they couldn't
- Make it worth staying

FOLLOW-UP:
- Create natural next steps
- Link to deeper content
- Reward continued engagement
