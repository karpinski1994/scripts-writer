HERO'S JOURNEY - The Classic Transformation Arc

CALL TO ADVENTURE:
- Introduce the hero in their ordinary world
- Present a catalyst that disrupts normalcy
- Establish stakes if they don't act

REFUSAL OF THE CALL:
- Show initial resistance or doubt
- Acknowledge fears and obstacles
- Present logical reasons to stay

MEETING THE MENTOR:
- Introduce guidance or transformation tool
- Share wisdom from experience
- Provide a map or framework

CROSSING THE THRESHOLD:
- Hero commits to the journey
- Leave the familiar behind
- Enter the new reality

TESTS, ALLIES, ENEMIES:
- Face challenges and obstacles
- Gain new perspectives
- Discover inner strength

ORDEAL:
- Darkest moment approaches
- Face the deepest fear
- Near defeat or death

REWARD:
- Claim the treasure/value
- Transform through the experience
- Gain new wisdom

RETURN WITH THE ELIXIR:
- Return to ordinary world changed
- Share the transformation
- Inspire others to follow
