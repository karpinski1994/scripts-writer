PAS - Problem, Agitation, Solution

PROBLEM:
- Identify the pain point directly
- Use the audience's own language
- Make it specific and concrete

AGITATION:
- Amplify the consequences
- Show what's at stake emotionally
- Highlight the cost of inaction
- Make the problem feel urgent

SOLUTION:
- Present the remedy clearly
- Show why it works
- Include proof or results
- Make next steps obvious
