AIDA - Attention, Interest, Desire, Action

ATTENTION:
- Lead with a bold claim or surprising statistic
- Ask a provocative question
- Use sensory language to paint a vivid scene

INTEREST:
- Build tension through relatable struggle
- Use specific details (names, numbers, dates)
- Create intrigue with what's at stake

DESIRE:
- Show the transformation (before vs after)
- Paint the emotional payoff
- Demonstrate social proof

ACTION:
- Make the next step effortless
- Create urgency with time-sensitive elements
- Remove friction from taking action
